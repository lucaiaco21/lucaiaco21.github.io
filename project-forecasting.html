The Challenge

What needed fixing

TCR Group rents Ground Support Equipment (GSE) to airlines and airports worldwide. To choose at what price the the equipment should be rent, the commercial team used an excel where the final price was done by a series of average of the past years and some other additional conditions inside the formulas.

The process is extremely slow because each commercial associate needed to find similar equipments, manually add all the information required and then check the final suggested price

Also, the final price often was wromg because based on a simply average of the price of the previous years for a similar type of EQ without taking into consideration non linear relationship in the change of rental prices and also without the possibility to generalize on new type of EQs or geography to quick check possible revenues

The model aim to predict the expected annual revenue of an equipment based on several information such as geographic location, Gross Book Value, type of customer, model type and past usage.

The Approach

Revenue Forecasting Model

  • 01 EDA First an EDA was done to detect inconsistencies in the data such as: normalization of the distribution and detection of outliers to remove the EQ with a rental revenue with more than 3 standard deviations from the mean; ajuste the str variables to avoid duplicates due to mispelling errors
  • 02 Feature Engineering & Encoding

    Feature Engineering to keep only the variables with an high correlation with the Y (Monthly Revenue) in order to avoid too much noise during the predition. After this, some variables, that were using similar information, were used together such as the Airport and Owning Entity or the EQ Model and the type of fuel used.

    Since the text feature were too many to just use the categorical support parameter of the mode, the 3 text variables were put together in one column (because they were a mix of customer + model + airport) and with OneHotEncoder library were transformed to only numerical data .

  • 03 Model Selection with Pycaret With the usage of Pycaret adn the compare_models() function, multiple algorithms were trained and the result was a ranked leaderboardto identify the best performer quickly (XGBoost).
  • 04 Hyperparameter Tuning

    Then the model chosen has been tuned through the usage of a Randomize Search CV. Here with param_grid were defined the hyperparameters to test but instead of trying all combinations (GridSearch), RandomizedSearchCV picks 50 random combinations from these options to finds the best one

  • 05 Interface Creation w/ Streamlit Creation of an interface to allow the commercial team to easily have prediction by just adding the information. A pipeline run after the click on run predictions and the fill in of all the info that use the same encoder and preprocessing of data done during the training
